Ames Data Documentation:

MSSubClass: Classifies the type of property for sale.

LotFrontage: The number of square feet connected to the property.

LotArea: Lot size in square feet.

OverallQual: Rating of the overall quality of the property.

OverallCond: Rating of the overall condition of the property.

YearBuilt: Year property was built.

YearRemodAdd: Year of property remodel.

BsmntFinSF1: Finished basement square footage (Type 1).

BsmntFinSF2: Finished basement square footage (Type 2).

BsmntUnfSF: Unfinished basement square footage.

TotalBsmtSF: Total square footage of the basement.

X1stFlrSF: First floor square footage.

X2ndFlrSF: Second floor square footage.

LowQualFinSF: Low quality finished square footage.

GrLivArea: Ground living area square footage.

BsmntFullBath: Basement full baths.

BsmntHalfBath: Basement half baths.

FullBath: Full baths above ground.

HalfBath: Half baths above ground.

BedroomAbvGr: Bedrooms above ground.

KitchenAbvGr: Kitchens above ground.

TotRmsAbvGrd: Total rooms above ground.

Fireplaces: Number of fireplaces.

GarageYrBlt: Year garage was built.

GarageCars: Car capacity of garage.

GarageArea: Garage square footage.

WoodDeckSF: Wood deck square footage.

OpenPorchSF: Open porch square footage.

EnclosedPorch: Enclosed porch square footage.

X3SsnPorch: Three season porch square footage.

ScreenPorch: Screen porch square footage.

PoolArea: Pool area in square feet.

MoSold: Month sold (MM).

YrSold: Year sold (YYYY).

SalePrice: Sale price of the property.
